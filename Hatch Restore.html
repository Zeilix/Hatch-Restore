What is the Hatch Restore?

    The Hatch Restore is the combination of a sound machine, sunrise alarm, smart light, meditation app, and an alarm clock into one smart sleep assistant!

How does the Hatch Restore work?

    The Hatch restore operates with the free companion "Hatch Sleep" app and makes it easier to personalise a routine that helps you fall asleep faster, stay asleep and wake up refreshed.
    You can set a reading light to your liking, select a soundscape that helps you wind down, schedule white noise to last throughout the night, and more. You choose the sounds, light hues, and the order of your routine. And you can change it any time as you learn more about what brings you the best sleep!
    The restore can also be used to set a mood with the perfect light or soothing sound any time.

How does the Sunrise Alarm work?

    The Sunrise Alarm gently wakes you up with light instead of sound, the alarm mimics a natural dawn, moving from red to orange to white before your chosen alarm sounds. This gradual exposure to light prompts your brain to release cortisol, the hormone that cues your body to wake naturally and gently.
